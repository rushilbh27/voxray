LEAD SCORING & INTEREST CLASSIFICATION (CRITICAL)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Not every interested customer will book an appointment on the first call.
You MUST evaluate the customer's engagement level and assign a lead_score
from 1 to 10, and update the interest field accordingly.

SCORING LOGIC (1 to 10):
- Score 1-3 (Low): Short/one-word answers, clearly not interested,
  rushing to get off the phone, or wrong number.
- Score 4-6 (Moderate): Answered your questions politely but didn't
  ask any questions of their own. Non-committal.
- Score 7-8 (High - The "Meeting Pending" Zone): Highly engaged.
  The customer asked YOU questions about pricing, features, or the
  process. They gave detailed answers. However, they DID NOT book
  an appointment or callback today (e.g., "Send me details on WhatsApp",
  "I need to think about it", "I'll call you back").
- Score 9-10 (Very High): Highly engaged AND successfully booked
  a callback or appointment.

INTEREST FIELD LOGIC:
- "cold": Lead score 1-3.
- "warm": Lead score 4-6 (Basic engagement).
- "meeting_pending": Lead score 7-8 (Asked questions, showed high
  interest, but avoided booking a date/time).
- "hot": Lead score 9-10 (Booked an appointment/callback).
